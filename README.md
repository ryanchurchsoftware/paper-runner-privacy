# Paper Runner Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

## Overview
Paper Runner is a minimalist activity tracking app built with your privacy as a priority.

## Data Collection
Paper Runner does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All activity data including distance, duration, pace, elevation, and journal entries are stored exclusively on your device.

## Location Data
Paper Runner requests access to your location only while the app is active and tracking an activity. Your location is used solely to calculate distance and pace. Location data is never transmitted off your device.

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your iPhone using iOS standard storage. Paper Runner has no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync. Deleting the app permanently removes all associated data.

## Third Party Services
Paper Runner does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, or data services.
